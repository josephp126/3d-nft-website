About

The Labyrinthine Unreal is a unique blend of interactive theory fiction,
exitentialist role play, and Labyrinthine puzzzle solving based within the
Ethereum blockchain with the addition of scripted elements that can
encroach upon reality as well. A fully customizable experience, the
Labyrinthine Unreal invites players to explore and advance at their own
pace, permitting opportunities to shop its treasure laden rooms at their
hearts desire.
